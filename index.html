History of Our Buses

1940s: The Beginning

1945

Our journey began in the 1940s with a small fleet of buses that served the local community. These buses were the first step in what would become a long tradition of reliable and safe transportation.

1960s: Expansion and Growth

1963

During the 1960s, we expanded our fleet and routes, introducing new and modern buses that improved comfort and efficiency. This era marked significant growth for our company.

1980s: Innovation and Modernization

1985

The 1980s brought technological advancements to our fleet, including the introduction of more fuel-efficient buses and improved safety features. We continued to innovate to meet the needs of our passengers.

2000s: A New Era of Sustainability

2005

In the 2000s, we committed to sustainability by introducing eco-friendly buses that reduced our carbon footprint. This move was part of our ongoing effort to protect the environment while providing excellent service.

2020s: The Future of Transportation

2023

Today, we continue to lead the way with state-of-the-art buses that feature the latest technology, ensuring that our passengers enjoy a safe, comfortable, and efficient journey every time.
